P78 — Orbital Logo (Constellation)

A constellation of satellites, each on its own real Keplerian orbit (body at the focus). Spread the orbital elements to sculpt the fan; cluster the apogees to one side for asymmetry.
Each orbit is a true ellipse with the body at the focus. “Apogee spread” fans the far ends; high eccentricity knots the near ends → one-sided wave.
